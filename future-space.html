Upcoming Technologies

  • Space Tourism: Companies like SpaceX and Blue Origin are making space travel accessible to civilians.
  • Mars Colonization: NASA and SpaceX plan to send humans to Mars by the 2030s.
  • Reusable Rockets: Advancements in reusable rocket technology are reducing the costs of space travel.
  • Interstellar Probes: Projects like Breakthrough Starshot aim to send tiny probes to other star systems.

Future Missions

Upcoming missions to explore our solar system and beyond include:

  • Artemis Program: Aiming to return humans to the Moon by 2024 and establish a sustainable presence.
  • James Webb Space Telescope: Launching soon to study distant galaxies and the formation of stars.
  • Europa Clipper: Scheduled to launch in 2024, it will investigate Jupiter's moon Europa for potential habitability.
