What is Space-Time?

Space-time is the fabric of the universe, where space and time are intertwined in a four-dimensional continuum. Discover how it shapes the cosmos!

The Fabric of Space-Time

Imagine space-time as a stretched fabric. Objects with mass, like planets and stars, create dents in this fabric, leading to gravitational effects. The greater the mass, the more pronounced the curvature.
