Exoplanets: Worlds Beyond Our Solar System

Exoplanets are planets that orbit stars outside our solar system. Join us on a journey to discover these alien worlds!

What Are Exoplanets?

An exoplanet is a planet that orbits a star outside the solar system. Over 4,000 exoplanets have been discovered, and the search for more continues!

Fun Facts About Exoplanets

  • Exoplanets come in a wide variety of types, some similar to Earth and others completely unlike any planets in our solar system.
  • The first confirmed discovery of an exoplanet was in 1992.
  • Some exoplanets may even have moons that could harbor life!
  • The Kepler Space Telescope has helped discover over 2,700 exoplanets since its launch.
